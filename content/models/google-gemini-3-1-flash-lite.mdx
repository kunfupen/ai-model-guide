## When to reach for Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite

Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite is Google's most cost-efficient Gemini model, part of the
natively multimodal Gemini 3 series. It entered preview on March 3, 2026 (Gemini
API in Google AI Studio, and Vertex AI for enterprises), with the stable
`gemini-3.1-flash-lite` release following on May 7, 2026.

- High-volume, latency-sensitive tasks: translation, classification, extraction.
- Cost-sensitive LLM traffic where 2.0/2.5 Flash-Lite quality wasn't enough —
  Google positions it as matching Gemini 2.5 Flash across key capability areas.
- Multimodal pipelines on a budget: it accepts text, images, audio, and video
  input with text output.

## Specs

- 1M-token input context window, up to 64k output tokens.
- $0.25 / 1M input, $1.50 / 1M output.
- Input: text, image, audio, video. Output: text.

## Tradeoffs

- Not the tier for hard reasoning or long-horizon agents — step up to Gemini 3.1
  Pro or 3.5 Flash for those.
- The `gemini-3.1-flash-lite-preview` endpoint was deprecated May 11, 2026 and
  shut down May 25, 2026; use the stable model ID.
