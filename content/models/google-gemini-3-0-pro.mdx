## When to reach for Gemini 3.0 Pro

Gemini 3.0 Pro is the successor to the 2.5 Pro family — Google's flagship across
reasoning, coding, and multimodal tasks. The headline change is the 2M-token context
window (double 2.5 Pro's), plus continued improvements to native multimodality and
long-horizon reasoning.

- Tasks that exceeded 1M tokens of context in 2.5 Pro — entire monorepos, multi-hour
  video transcripts, large document collections.
- Heavy reasoning or agentic workloads where 2.5 Pro felt borderline.
- Multimodal pipelines mixing video, audio, and text in a single call.

For high-volume or latency-sensitive paths, Gemini 3.0 Flash (or sticking with 2.5
Flash) is still the right call.

## Prompt patterns that work well

- **Lean on long context, but structure it.** Even at 2M tokens, prompt ordering
  matters — task statement first, documents middle, task restated at the end.
- **Use the reasoning controls.** Gemini 3 exposes thinking-budget controls; set
  them based on whether the request is interactive or batch.
- **Mix modalities deliberately.** Video + text reasoning works well; describe what
  you want grounded in the video versus answered from general knowledge.

## Long-context notes

Per-token cost is the same regardless of input size up to 2M, but latency and time-
to-first-token grow with input length. For interactive use cases over ~500k tokens,
prefer Flash or pre-filter with retrieval.
