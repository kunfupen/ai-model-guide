## When to reach for Gemini 2.5 Flash

Flash is the sweet spot of the Gemini lineup: same 1M-token context window as Pro at
a fraction of the price. For long-context tasks that don't need Pro's reasoning depth,
it's hard to beat on cost-per-token.

- Bulk summarization or extraction over large corpora.
- Retrieval-augmented pipelines where you'd rather pay for context than embed
  cleverness in retrieval logic.
- High-volume multimodal classification (images, short audio clips).

## Prompt patterns that work well

- **Bias toward structure.** Flash follows JSON / tagged output reliably and is faster
  to parse downstream.
- **Skip elaborate few-shot.** It tends to follow concise instructions better than
  long example chains at this tier.
- **Pre-filter with retrieval anyway.** Even with 1M context, prompt cost is linear
  in input size — use retrieval to feed only the relevant chunks when you can.

## Cost intuition

At ~$0.30 input / $2.50 output per million tokens, a 100k-token RAG prompt costs
about three cents. That's the price point where "just stuff the docs into context"
becomes a serious alternative to building a vector store.
