## When to reach for Claude Mythos

Claude Mythos is Anthropic's frontier "Mythos-class" model, revealed in April 2026
as a research preview. It performs strongly across the board but is strikingly
capable at **computer-security tasks** — early partners used it to find more than
10,000 high- or critical-severity flaws and thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities.

> **Preview — not generally available.** Mythos is offered only through the gated
> **Project Glasswing** program (founding partners + critical-infrastructure
> organizations), via the Claude API, Amazon Bedrock, Google Vertex AI, or
> Microsoft Foundry. There is no public waitlist or self-service access, and no
> announced GA date — though Anthropic has said it expects to bring Mythos-class
> models to all customers "in the coming weeks."
