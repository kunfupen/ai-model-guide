## When to reach for Claude Mythos 5

Claude Mythos 5 is the unguarded sibling of [Claude Fable 5](/models/anthropic-claude-fable-5),
released the same day (June 9, 2026). It's the **same underlying model at the same
price** — the difference is that Mythos 5 ships **without** the safety classifiers
that Fable applies to sensitive domains.

Because of that, Mythos 5 is **not generally available**: access is limited to
**Project Glasswing** partners and U.S. government cyber defenders. If you're a
general developer, [Fable 5](/models/anthropic-claude-fable-5) is the version you
can actually use, with identical capabilities and pricing.

## Fable vs. Mythos

- **Same model, same $10 / $50 pricing, same 1M context** (128K max output).
- **Fable 5** adds guardrails for cyber, bio/chem, and model-extraction queries
  (routing sensitive requests to an Opus 4.8 fallback); they trigger in under 5%
  of sessions.
- **Mythos 5** removes those guardrails — hence the restricted, gated availability.

## Specs & cost

- **Context:** 1M tokens, up to 128K output.
- **Inputs:** text, images, and files; text output.
- **Thinking:** adaptive only — use the `effort` parameter to control depth.
- **Pricing:** $10 / 1M input and $50 / 1M output.
- **Availability:** limited release via Project Glasswing only.
