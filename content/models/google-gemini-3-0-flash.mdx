## When to reach for Gemini 3 Flash

Gemini 3 Flash launched December 17, 2025 as the speed tier of the Gemini 3
family — Pro-grade reasoning at Flash-level latency, efficiency, and cost, at
less than a quarter the cost of Gemini 3 Pro with higher rate limits. It rolled
out globally at launch in the Gemini app and AI Mode in Search, and to developers
via the Gemini API in Google AI Studio, Google Antigravity, Gemini CLI, Android
Studio, Vertex AI, and Gemini Enterprise.

- The default high-throughput pick of the Gemini 3 generation: fast, cheap, and
  strong at coding — on SWE-bench Verified it scores 78%, outperforming both the
  2.5 series and Gemini 3 Pro.
- Agentic coding loops where per-call cost and latency compound.
- Multimodal work: natively accepts text, images, audio, and video input.

## Specs

- 1M-token input context window, up to 64k output tokens.
- $0.50 / 1M input, $3 / 1M output (audio input $1 / 1M).
- Google reports it outperforms 2.5 Pro while being roughly 3x faster (Artificial
  Analysis benchmarking).

## Tradeoffs

- Superseded on some axes by the newer 3.1 and 3.5 series (3.5 Flash is now the
  model behind `gemini-flash-latest`); reach for those tiers if you want the
  current frontier.
