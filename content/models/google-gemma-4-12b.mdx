## When to reach for Gemma 4 12B

Gemma 4 12B is Google's open-weight model (released June 3, 2026) that brings
capable multimodal AI to a laptop: it runs locally with just ~16GB of VRAM or
unified memory, under an **Apache 2.0** license.

- Self-hosting, privacy, and on-device agents without a cloud dependency.
- Multimodal input — text, images, and audio — with a novel **encoder-free
  unified architecture** (vision/audio flow directly into the LLM backbone).
- Long-context local work: a 256K-token window, unusual for a model this size.

For frontier-grade quality, Google's hosted Gemini models are stronger; Gemma is
the "run it yourself" option.

## Specs

- **Parameters:** ~11.95B, 48 layers, hybrid local/global attention (1024-token
  sliding window, final layer always global).
- **Context:** 256K tokens; 262K vocabulary.
- **Inputs:** text, image, audio.
- **License:** Apache 2.0 (open weights on Hugging Face, Ollama, LM Studio).

> No per-token pricing — it's an open-weight model you host yourself (hosted
> API options vary by provider). GPQA Diamond (78.8, instruction-tuned variant)
> comes from the official Gemma 4 Technical Report; MMLU / HumanEval / SWE-bench
> Verified figures aren't published there — omitted rather than guessed.
