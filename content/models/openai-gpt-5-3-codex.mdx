## When to reach for GPT-5.3-Codex

GPT-5.3-Codex is OpenAI's most advanced agentic coding model — the first to
combine the Codex and GPT-5 training stacks, pairing frontier software-engineering
performance with broader reasoning and professional knowledge. It powers Codex
and is built for long-running, tool-using development work.

- Long-horizon coding agents: debugging, deployment, iterative product work.
- Tasks that benefit from interactive steering mid-execution.
- When you want a coding specialist rather than a general-purpose model — for
  mixed workloads, GPT-5.4 is the better all-rounder.

## Specs & cost

- **Context:** 400K tokens, 128K max output.
- **Reasoning effort:** low, medium, high, and xhigh settings.
