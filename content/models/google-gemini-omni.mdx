## When to reach for Gemini Omni

Gemini Omni, introduced at Google I/O on May 19, 2026, is Google DeepMind's
natively multimodal model: it processes and generates text, images, audio, and
video within a single core engine rather than routing between separate tools.
Its standout capability is handling **video as both input and output** — a full
multimodal pipeline rather than one-directional generation.

- Creating dynamic video content by blending text, audio, image, and video inputs.
- Social and broadcast pipelines — it generates native 9:16 vertical, 1:1 square,
  and 16:9 widescreen framing.
- Unified multimodal understanding where you'd otherwise stitch several models
  together. The lighter **Gemini Omni Flash** targets faster, cheaper rollouts
  (already live across the Gemini app, Google Flow, and YouTube Shorts).

## Specs & availability

- **Modalities:** native text, image, audio, and video — in and out.
- **Availability:** Gemini API / Google AI Studio, the Gemini app and Google Flow,
  with Vertex AI typically following a week or two after consumer launch.
