## When to reach for Gemini 3.5 Flash

Gemini 3.5 Flash is Google's newest lightweight model, unveiled at Google I/O 2026 and
made the default for the Gemini app and AI Mode in Search globally. It targets the sweet
spot the Flash tier is known for: frontier-grade reasoning at a fraction of the cost and
latency of the largest models.

- High-volume, latency-sensitive workloads — chat, classification, extraction, routing.
- Agentic and coding tasks where you want strong quality but can't pay frontier prices.
- Long, multimodal inputs: it keeps the 1M-token context window and native text, image,
  audio, and video understanding.

It posts surprisingly strong reasoning numbers for its tier — the Gemini 3 Flash line
already hit ~90% on GPQA Diamond, rivaling much larger models. For the very hardest
problems, step up to a Pro-tier model (Gemini 3.5 Pro is rolling out shortly after Flash).

## Prompt patterns that work well

- **Put the task first.** With large multimodal context, lead with a one-line task
  statement, then the documents/media, then restate the task.
- **Use thinking selectively.** Budget extra reasoning only for the prompts that need
  it; Flash is fast and cheap precisely because most prompts don't.
- **Ground with Search.** Enable grounding when answers depend on fresh, citable facts.
