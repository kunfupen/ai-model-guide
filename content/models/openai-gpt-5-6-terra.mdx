## When to reach for GPT-5.6 Terra

Terra is the balanced mid-tier of the GPT-5.6 family (GA July 9, 2026) — built
for production workloads that need strong quality at sensible cost. It sits
between the flagship **Sol** and the high-throughput **Luna**.

- The everyday production workhorse for chat, coding, and agents.
- Workloads where Sol's quality edge doesn't justify 2× the price.
- Long-context work: the full ~1M-token window comes at this tier too.

## Specs & cost

- **Context:** ~1.05M tokens, 128K max output (shared across the 5.6 family).
- **Pricing:** $2.50 / 1M input and $15 / 1M output. Cached-input reads are 90%
  cheaper; cache writes bill at 1.25× input.

> did not publish academic benchmarks at launch. MMLU / HumanEval / SWE-bench
> Verified have no credible published figures — omitted rather than guessed.
