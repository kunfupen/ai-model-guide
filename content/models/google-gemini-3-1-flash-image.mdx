## When to reach for Gemini 3.1 Flash Image

Gemini 3.1 Flash Image is Google's state-of-the-art image generation and editing
model, now generally available. It targets the Flash sweet spot — Pro-level
visual quality at Flash speed and cost — for quick, interactive image work and
high-throughput pipelines.

- Fast, cost-efficient image generation and editing.
- Contextual edits that need real-world grounding from Gemini.
- Video-to-image generation (thumbnails, posters, infographics).

For the highest-fidelity output, step up to **Gemini 3.1 Pro Image**; for unified
text/image/audio/video generation, see **Gemini Omni**.

## Specs & cost

- **Context:** 131,072 tokens, up to 65,536 output tokens.
- **Pricing is per-image, not per-token**, so it's omitted from the spec fields
  above. As published: ~$0.0006 per input image; output scales by resolution —
  ~$0.045 (512px), ~$0.067 (1K), ~$0.101 (2K), ~$0.15 (4K). Confirm on the
  pricing page.
- **Availability:** Gemini API / AI Studio, the Gemini app, and Vertex AI.
