## When to reach for Claude Sonnet 5

Claude Sonnet 5 (released June 30, 2026) is the first Sonnet of the Claude 5
generation and, in Anthropic's words, **"the most agentic Sonnet."** It closes
most of the gap to Opus 4.8 at a fraction of the price — early testers report it
finishes complex tasks where earlier Sonnets stopped short and checks its own
work unprompted.

- The default workhorse for production chat, coding, and agent pipelines — it's
  now the default model on Claude Free and Pro plans.
- Agentic coding on a budget: it **beats Opus 4.8 on Terminal-Bench 2.1** (80.4
  vs 71.9, same harness) — the first mid-tier Claude to out-score the flagship
  on a suite benchmark.
- Long-context work: the 1M-token window comes standard.

Escalate to Fable 5 or Opus 4.8 only for the prompts that genuinely need
frontier depth.

## Specs & cost

- **Context:** 1M tokens standard.
- **Pricing:** $3 / 1M input and $15 / 1M output — with **introductory pricing
  of $2 / $10 through August 31, 2026**.
- **Availability:** Claude API/Platform, claude.ai (default on Free/Pro), Claude
  Code, Amazon Bedrock, and Vertex AI.

## Benchmark notes

SWE-bench Verified (85.2) and Terminal-Bench 2.1 (80.4) are corroborated
leaderboard figures — both huge jumps over Sonnet 4.6, and it beats GPT-5.5 on
directly comparable benchmarks at lower cost. GPQA Diamond is omitted: only a
"GPQA-AAA v2" variant score is published, which isn't the same benchmark. MMLU /
HumanEval / τ²-bench figures aren't published yet.
