## When to reach for GPT-5.6 Sol

Sol is the flagship of OpenAI's GPT-5.6 family (GA July 9, 2026, after a
limited preview from late June), setting a new standard for both intelligence and
efficiency with state-of-the-art results across coding, knowledge work,
cybersecurity, and science.

- The hardest frontier reasoning, agentic, and scientific tasks.
- Long-horizon work that benefits from the ~1M-token context window.
- Its new **"ultra" mode** pushes even harder on a task and can delegate work to
  submodels.

For balanced production workloads, **GPT-5.6 Terra** is the mid-tier; for
high-volume, latency-sensitive paths, **GPT-5.6 Luna** is the cheap/fast option.

## Specs & cost

- **Context:** ~1.05M tokens, 128K max output (shared across the 5.6 family).
- **Pricing:** $5 / 1M input and $30 / 1M output. Cached-input reads are 90%
  cheaper; cache writes bill at 1.25× input.
- **Note:** GPT-5.4 is being retired (July 23) following the 5.6 launch.
